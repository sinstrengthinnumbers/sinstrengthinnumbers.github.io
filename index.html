Server Rules

General Rules

  • This is a PVE only server. Intentional killing of another player may result in a permanent ban. Use F11 if you need to respawn.
  • There is zero tolerance for racism, sexism, homophobia, or harassment of any kind. No toxic chat or behavior. If it might offend another player, do not say or do it!
  • No theft. This includes but is not limited to, vehicles, items stored in vehicles, bases or body bags. Ask around if you find items on the ground that may belong to others.
  • Respect events. Listen to Marshals/Admins.
  • No glitching, exploiting game bugs, nesting items, duping, hacking or otherwise trying to gain an unfair advantage.
  • No loot cycling, this is defined as removing low-value or unwanted items from their spawn point and discarding them in a container or on the ground in an attempt to force new loot to spawn.
  • Compensation for lost, ruined or de-spawned items or vehicles is at the discretion of the admin and they reserve the right to refuse.
  • English only when using game chat.

    • Vehicle Rules

  • Cars and helis do not spawn on the main server, if you did not buy it, leave it alone.
  • Traders and safe zones are for loading & unloading only. Any vehicles left at the trader longer than 30 minutes may be impounded.
  • Vehicles left on the map will despawn after 48 hours, territory flags will not protect your vehicle. Use of the virtual garage is highly encouraged - at all times.
  • Vehicles on Dayz can be glitchy and should be only used at your own risk. Lag from your internet connection greatly affects this!
  • No Fly/Drive Zone: Paris Island, Temple, Crater, Area42. Vehicles are not allowed and will get Deleted.

    • Base Rules

  • You must have a flag to build a base. Flagpoles cannot be dismantled, so choose your location wisely.
  • Only one flagpole/base per person or team.
  • Your flag will descend over 7 days. Bases will be deleted when they reach 0 (all the way down).
  • Only one vanilla building may be blocked or secured.
  • Panel count is limited to 30 panels per solo base. +10 panels for additional player. 50 panel maximum. Each foundation, floor, fence or wall count as 1 panel. Cabins count as 20 panels.
  • Do not build on or in roadways, hospitals or clinics , gas stations, fire stations, police stations, schools, wood spawns, cement mixers, water wells, or Military Areas./li>
  • Building height is not to exceed 3 panels in height.
  • All bases must be in direct contact with the ground and may not appear unsupported and free floating.
  • 30 Base storage units per solo base, +5 base storage units for additional player. 50 units maximum.
  • Maximum of 2 tents per base.

    • Black Market/Hidden Traders/Secret Events

  • Do not talk about black market, hidden traders and secret events outside of your group.
  • Do not sell information or share information outside your group. Finding the locations is a experience for people to experience on their own.
